About Me, The "Eternal Scholar"

Yea, it sounds cheesy, but if you were to have me assign a title to myself, that would be it. I am of the mind that "To live, is to learn", and I believe that there is always someway to improve yourself. I love to learn and I love to learn about everything. This blog is mostly about my adventrures in Dev BootCamp(DBC) currently and hopefully it will eventually branch out to other topics in my life.

My number one goal right now is to be a great developer and create something that will change the world. A big dream, I know, but I believe having big dreams make the journey that much greater. The time I'm having in DBC is teaching me a world of new things and this step is going to be huge in reaching my goal. Technology is such an important part of our lives and I feel like there are many unexplored ways to make it even better. I'm hoping to learn even more once I finish DBC and expand my knowledge of how technology impacts society. My dream is to make something everyone in the world will know about! I want to make something great!

I grew up in NYC as a relatively shy boy. I wasn't sure what I wanted to do when I grew up. I had family members throwing me ideas and medicine stuck out a lot for me. The feeling of being able to help people really felt like me. I started on the path to medicine and as I got older, I realized it wasn't the thing for me. I wanted to help people still, but I don't think medicine let my creative side out. I went into business for a little while to sort of test the waters, but I don't think having the dollar be the deciding factor for many of the decisions I would have to make sit well with me. I finally learned about coding and realized that "This is it", this is how I can do something creative and at the same time affect people around the world.

Hobbies, I love mine. I get very analytical with my hobbies as well, much to my friends displeasure. I love to play video games, basketball, and nowadays, code. My favorite series of video games is probably Final Fantasy and it has been with me since I was growing up. I also love to play and watch basketball. I could use some work on my jumper, I think its getting rusty. Coding has been my main thing to do since I started DBC. I enjoying working through the challenges and finding multiple ways to reach the solutions. I hope to learn more about coding in the days to come!
